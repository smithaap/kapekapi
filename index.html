The Soul of Kape Kapi

Kape Kapi & Exports is a new but experienced player in the coffee export market, built on 15 years of industry expertise. Operating out of Kushalnagar, Karnataka, we maintain a state-of-the-art coffee curing facility and source raw coffee directly from farmers, avoiding middlemen entirely.

We support certification processes like Organic, Rainforest Alliance, 4C, and Fairtrade to ensure ethical farming practices and product integrity. With 5000 Mt of certified cleaned coffee and comprehensive traceability from farm to cup, we proudly supply leading multinational companies including Nestle, ITC, Olam, NKG, Ecom, LDC, and Allana Sons.

Our reach extends across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa with year-round supply capabilities.
